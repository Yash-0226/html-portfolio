Angela's Recipe for the best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 3/4 cujp warm milk
  • 2 teaspoons yeast
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling

  • 2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1.5 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrapping. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
